Before I Die Wall

About a year ago, I read a coffee table book about Before I Die Walls. I recommend clicking on the previous link to learn more, but in short, a Before I Die Wall is giant chalkboard with spaces for people to finish the sentence "Before I Die I Want to...". A public bucket list and art project in one. The idea is to encourage communities across the globe to contemplate mortality and share both common and personal fears, ambitions, hopes, and dreams. I fell in love with the idea and the responses I read from existing walls, and was inspired to create my own for our local community. After winning a monthly pitch contest, I decided to follow through with the idea.


On this page you'll find photos of the finished product. It made its debut during the Central Pennsylvania Festival of the Arts and received hundreds of response - from thoughtful and introspective to heavy-handed, with plenty in-between. If you're finding it difficult to read some of the writing from a photo, click it for a high-rez version.
